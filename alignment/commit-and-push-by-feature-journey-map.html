Journey Map Alignment

Preview of the new research/journey-map.md artifact for GappHub's anonymous visitor flow from discovery trigger to product click-through.

Purpose

Define the portfolio launcher journey so metrics, GTM, monetization, and landing-copy work stay tied to actual visitor behavior.

Positioning Guardrail

Keep GappHub framed as a branded product portfolio launcher, not a marketplace, app store, commerce surface, or standalone SaaS.

Implementation Scope

Documentation only. The artifact records analytics and infrastructure as future measurement ideas, not current build requirements.

1Trigger

Visitor opens a social bio, shared URL, pitch-deck link, search result, QR code, or product cross-link.

2Orientation

The boot sequence and phone frame communicate Lexcorp portfolio breadth and polish.

3Scan

The visitor reads icon names, badges, page dots, dock entries, and search affordances.

4Narrow

An app-style drawer gives enough product context for a confident choice.

5Click

The Open CTA sends the visitor to the selected product site.

6Explore

The visitor can close the drawer and continue comparing products.
