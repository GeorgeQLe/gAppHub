GappHub Positioning Alignment

Preview of the canonical research artifact written to research/positioning.md.

Positioning: GappHub is a branded product portfolio launcher for visitors, partners, investors, and candidates who need a fast, credible way to understand the breadth and quality of Lexcorp's product portfolio.

Unlike generic link-in-bio tools, scattered product sites, or template portfolio pages, GappHub uses a bespoke iPhone home-screen metaphor, app-style drawer details, and self-hosted product data to make the portfolio itself feel like a shipped Lexcorp product.

Recommended Category

Branded product portfolio launcher. This keeps the product understandable as adjacent to link-in-bio and portfolio pages without inheriting marketplace, app-store, creator commerce, or standalone SaaS expectations.

Core Evidence

ICP

Visitors need one shareable URL, fast scanning, and immediate credibility.

Competitive Analysis

The market is crowded with generic or creator-monetization tools, not company portfolio launchers.

Concept Brief

The value wedge is turning a link list into a memorable Apple-inspired showcase.

UI Spec

The phone frame, app grid, search, dock, and drawers make the metaphor concrete.

Implications

  • Lead copy with the portfolio role: one shareable front door for Lexcorp products.
  • Keep app-store cues as interaction language, not market category language.
  • Avoid claims around marketplace, commerce, ratings, installs, accounts, or personalization.
  • Use the phone frame as the first-viewport proof point because the metaphor is the positioning.
