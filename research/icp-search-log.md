# ICP Search Log — GappHub

> Lightweight ICP research log. 8 web searches conducted 2026-05-21.

## Search Queries & Findings

### 1. "who uses branded link hub portfolio page Linktree Bento bio link audience"

**Purpose:** Identify who uses bio-link / portfolio hub products and why.

**Key findings:**
- Bento (now shut down) focused on artists, designers, photographers, web developers, and streamers
- Linktree serves quick, dependable setup; Beacons serves creators wanting commerce and email capture
- Bio-link platforms serve content creators, professionals, artists, brands, and freelancers centralizing online presence
- Sources: Medium (Design Bootcamp), own.page, Product Hunt

### 2. "product portfolio showcase page B2B visitors use cases"

**Purpose:** Understand B2B visitor needs for product showcase pages.

**Key findings:**
- Product showcase websites present features, benefits, and use cases — demonstrating value rather than describing it
- Segmented content discovery helps visitors self-select based on their role
- Interactive demos and use case visualizations help visitors see product fit
- Design should balance copy with imagery, with clear calls-to-action
- Sources: Guideflow, Bluleadz, Kompass

### 3. "bio link page visitor behavior what users want 2025 2026"

**Purpose:** Understand current visitor behavior and expectations on bio-link pages.

**Key findings:**
- Bio-link CTR averages ~4.7% on social profiles (2026 benchmark)
- 53% of mobile users abandon pages taking >3 seconds to load
- Shift from navigation to conversion — creators treat bio links as mini landing pages
- Visitors want clear goals, clean structure, and guided flow
- Sources: LinkDrip, Stan Store, Biolinkd, beehiiv

### 4. "multi-product company portfolio page best practices visitor needs"

**Purpose:** Best practices for presenting multiple products to visitors.

**Key findings:**
- Clear navigation and filtering help visitors sort through work by interest
- Balance copy with imagery; large images pull focus to projects
- Brief tags (like "product," "UI design," "brand") showcase multiple arenas
- SEO optimization with relevant keywords in project titles and descriptions
- Sources: Webflow, site123.com, Hanover Research

### 5. "product showcase portfolio hub visitor types investors partners customers"

**Purpose:** Identify distinct visitor types for portfolio/showcase pages.

**Key findings:**
- Portfolio hubs serve multiple visitor types: customers, investors, partners, internal teams
- Product showcase features attract buyers, enable due diligence for investors
- Portfolio pages serve as credibility signals for business development
- Sources: Eventify, HostAdvice, Dragonboat

### 6. "startup portfolio page who visits why investors partners job candidates"

**Purpose:** Understand non-customer visitor segments (investors, talent).

**Key findings:**
- 86% of hiring decision-makers visit portfolios when available
- 71% of employers say portfolio quality influences hiring decisions
- VCs use portfolio pages for evaluating team, traction, and investment opportunities
- Partners use them to understand capabilities and identify collaboration opportunities
- Sources: Format.com, Wall Street Oasis, 4Degrees

### 7. "company product page visitor intent segments what visitors want from product listings"

**Purpose:** Understand visitor intent distribution.

**Key findings:**
- 80% informational intent, 10% transactional, 10% navigational
- Companies classify pages as low-, medium-, high-intent for follow-up strategy
- Visitors arrive with varying intent from "just browsing" to "ready to purchase"
- Behavioral segmentation by geography, behavior, characteristics, and acquisition source
- Sources: AMA, Clearbit, VWO

### 8. "tech company product catalog page analytics who visits bounce rate engagement"

**Purpose:** Understand engagement benchmarks for product pages.

**Key findings:**
- Low bounce rate target: under 40% for product/lead-gen pages
- GA4 engagement rate measures meaningful engagement percentage
- High bounce on product pages may indicate: high price, broken images, unclear descriptions
- Segmenting by channel reveals which sources drive most engaged users
- Sources: Google Analytics Help, Prospeo, Klipfolio

## Scoring Rationale

No formal scoring matrix was used (lightweight ICP mode). Segment prioritization based on:

1. **Prospective Customers & Users** — highest volume, most direct alignment with GappHub's purpose
2. **Partners & Investors** — highest per-visit value, GappHub serves as credibility proof
3. **Job Candidates & Talent** — growing importance, well-served by the existing design
